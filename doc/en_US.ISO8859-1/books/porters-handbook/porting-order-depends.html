15.6. The Dependencies Block

This block is optional. The variables are:

  • FETCH_DEPENDS

  • EXTRACT_DEPENDS

  • PATCH_DEPENDS

  • BUILD_DEPENDS

  • LIB_DEPENDS

  • RUN_DEPENDS

  • TEST_DEPENDS
