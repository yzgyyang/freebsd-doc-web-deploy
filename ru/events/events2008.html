    FreeBSD Past Events

    Если вы располагаете информацией о событиях, связанных с FreeBSD, или которые будут интересны пользователям FreeBSD, из отсутствующих здесь, то, пожалуйста, пришлите все подробности на адрес www@FreeBSD.org, чтобы эта информация оказалась здесь.

    Countries and regions displayed in dark red on the map below are hosting upcoming FreeBSD-related events. Countries that have hosted FreeBSD-related events in the past show up in yellow or orange, with darker colors representing a larger number of previous events.

    Прошлые события:

    Декабрь 2008

    • NLLGG-bijeenkomst december 2008, Liefland college, Utrecht, Utrecht, Нидерланды
      13 Декабрь, 2008
      The "Nederlandse Linux Gebruikers Groep" (NLLGG) organisation is arranging a national community day in Utrecht focussing on BSD. Robert Watson, Hans vd Looy, Marten Vijn, Wim Vandeputte and Remko Lodder will do presentations for the Linux community.

      Social links: Flickr, Blog Search.

    Ноябрь 2008

    • MeetBSD California 2008, Googleplex, Mountain View, California, США
      15 - 16 Ноябрь, 2008
      meetBSD was started in Krakow, Poland in 2004 and strong turnout and highly regarded technical presentations have allowed it to continue each year since then. meetBSD is a conference devoted to the BSD operating systems with a strong emphasis towards FreeBSD. meetBSD 2007 was held in Warsaw, Poland and will continue this year in Mountain View, California. This year marks the first meetBSD held in California, so join us to celebrate FreeBSD's 15th Anniversary!

      Social links: Flickr, Blog Search, YouTube.

    Октябрь 2008

    • Ticino Informatica 2008, Palazzo dei Congressi, Lugano, Швейцария
      22 - 25 Октябрь, 2008
      FreeBSD and The FreeBSD Foundation Stand A13 Presented by OEMS Sagl

      Social links: Flickr, Blog Search.

    • EuroBSDCon 2008, University of Strasbourg, Strasbourg, Франция
      18 - 19 Октябрь, 2008
      The seventh annual European BSD Conference. It is oriented to developers and users of all BSD flavors, including FreeBSD of course. A variety of full and half-day tutorials will precede the conference.

      Social links: Flickr, Blog Search.

    • NYCBSDCon 2008, Columbia University, New York City, США
      11 - 12 Октябрь, 2008
      The New York City *BSD Conference (NYCBSDCon) 2008 will be held at Columbia University in Manhattan. NYCBSDCon is the main technical conference on the East Coast for the BSD community to get together to share and gain knowledge, to network with like-minded people, and to have fun. This event is organized by members of the New York City *BSD Users Group (NYC*BUG).

      Social links: Flickr, Blog Search, YouTube.

    Сентябрь 2008

    • OpenExpo 2008, Zurich, Швейцария
      24 - 25 Сентябрь, 2008
      The goal of OpenExpo is the promotion of Open Source software in the business environment. Among many others, there will be a FreeBSD booth.

      Social links: Flickr, Blog Search.

    Август 2008

    • COSCUP 2008 - Conference for Open Source Coders, Users and Promoters, International Conference Hall, Institute of Applied Mechanics, National Taiwan University, Taipei, Тайвань
      23 - 24 Август, 2008
      COSCUP 2008 is the 3rd incarnation of the series, and becomes the biggest annual open source community party in Taiwan. Several FreeBSD contributions will be presenting on FreeBSD topics.

      Social links: Flickr, Blog Search.

    • FrOSCon 2008, Fachhochschule Bonn-Rhein-Sieg, Sankt Augustin, Германия
      23 - 24 Август, 2008
      FrOSCon is a two day conference on Free Software and Open Source, taking place in Sankt Augustin near the cities of Bonn and Cologne. The conference revolves around a rich schedule of talks, highlighting current topics in Free Software and Open Source.

      Social links: Flickr, Blog Search.

    Июль 2008

    • O'Reilly Open Source Conference 2008, Oregon Convention Center, Portland, Oregon, США
      21 - 25 Июль, 2008
      The annual O'Reilly Open Source Conference will again have a FreeBSD booth this year. Matt Olander, Dru Lavigne, and others will help spread the word about FreeBSD.

      Social links: Flickr, Blog Search.

    • 9th Libre Software Meeting, Multiple Locations, Mont-de-Marsan, Франция
      1 - 5 Июль, 2008
      The Libre Software Meeting has occurred each year since July 2000. This year there will again be a FreeBSD boot camp to introduce new users to the Operating System.

      Social links: Flickr, Blog Search.

    Май 2008

    • LinuxTag 2008, Messe Berlin, Berlin, Германия
      28 - 31 Май, 2008
      LinuxTag is Europe's largest event about Free Software, Linux and Open Source. This year there will be the traditional FreeBSD booth and several BSD talks. It will also be possible to take the BSDA certification exam there on Friday May 30.

      Social links: Flickr, Blog Search.

    • BSDCan 2008, University of Ottawa, Ottawa, Канада
      16 - 17 Май, 2008
      BSDCan - это конференция разработчиков с сильной ориентацией на передовые технологии, исследовательские проекты и находящиеся в разработке идеи. На ней также представлены инфраструктурные пользовательские проекты, к участию приглашаются разработчики как свободного программного обеспечения, так и коммерческих продуктов.

      Social links: Flickr, Blog Search.

    Апрель 2008

    • BSDCon Barcelona 2008, Universitat Politecnica de Catalunya, Barcelona, Испания
      19 - 20 Апрель, 2008
      BSDCon Barcelona is a conference on all BSD-based operating systems. Submissions can be on any topic, as long as they are somehow connected to BSD. There is room for all kinds of topics ranging from documentation to kernel-programming and using systems for various tasks.

      Social links: Flickr, Blog Search.

    • OSDC.tw 2008 - Open Source Developers' Conference in Taiwan, School Of Continuing Education Chinese Culture University, Taipei, Тайвань
      12 - 13 Апрель, 2008
      OSDC.tw is a major conference for open source developers and contributions. The subject of OSDC.tw 2008 is the "Innovation and Implementation". Several FreeBSD developers and contributions will be presenting on FreeBSD topics.

      Social links: Flickr, Blog Search.

    Март 2008

    • Linux-Infotag 2008, FH Augsburg, Augsburg, Германия
      29 Март, 2008
      The Augsburger Linux-Infotag offers two parallel tracks of talks to show the capabilities of free and open source software. There will be several talks about FreeBSD as well as a FreeBSD booth.

      Social links: Flickr, Blog Search.

    • AsiaBSDCon 2008, Tokyo University of Science, Tokyo, Япония
      27 - 30 Март, 2008
      AsiaBSDCon is a conference for users and developers on BSD based systems. The conference is for anyone developing, deploying and using systems based on FreeBSD, NetBSD, OpenBSD, DragonFlyBSD, Darwin and MacOS X. AsiaBSDCon is a technical conference and aims to collect the best technical papers and presentations available to ensure that the latest developments in our open source community are shared with the widest possible audience.

      Social links: Flickr, Blog Search.

    • Unigroup FreeBSD7 Launch Meeting, Alliance for Downtown NY Conference Facility, New York, США
      13 Март, 2008
      UNIGROUP of New York presents FreeBSD 7, the latest release of the FreeBSD Operating System. George Neville-Neil, a member of the FreeBSD Project will give a presentation of the main features and significant improvements in the latest release as well as talk about the FreeBSD project and how the operating system is developed. The presentation will be followed by a question and answer period.

      Social links: Flickr, Blog Search.

    • Linuxtage Chemnitz 2008, Technical University Chemnitz, Chemnitz, Германия
      1 - 2 Март, 2008
      Linuxtage Chemnitz is a two day event on Linux and Free Software in general. There will be workshops, talks and room for various projects to present themselves. FreeBSD will be present with a booth as well as several talks.

      Social links: Flickr, Blog Search.

    Февраль 2008

    • FOSDEM 2008, Universite Libre de Bruxelles, Brussels, Бельгия
      23 - 24 Февраль, 2008
      A 2-day event to promote the widespread use of Free and Open Source software. There will be a joined BSD and PostgreSQL developer room. Several FreeBSD developers will be present with Core Team member Robert Watson giving the opening talk on one of the conference days.

      Social links: Flickr, Blog Search.

    • SCALE: Southern California Linux Expo 2008, The Westin Los Angeles Airport, Los Angeles, California, США
      9 - 10 Февраль, 2008
      There will be a FreeBSD presence at the sixth Southern California Linux Expo in February. Matt Olander will be representing the project.

      Social links: Flickr, Blog Search.

    Январь 2008

    • Solutions Linux 2008, CNIT, Paris, Франция
      29 - 31 Январь, 2008
      A 3 day event to promote GNU/Linux and Open Source software. French FreeBSD User Group will be present in the Association Village to promote FreeBSD of course !

      Social links: Flickr, Blog Search.
